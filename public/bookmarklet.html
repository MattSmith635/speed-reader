Speed Reader

Bookmarklet Setup

Install

  1. Drag the button below to your bookmarks bar.
  2. Navigate to any article you want to speed-read.
  3. Click the Speed Read bookmark.
  4. The article will open in Speed Reader, ready to go.

Speed Read

Why a bookmarklet? It runs in your browser on the page you're already viewing, so it works with paywalled articles, login-gated content, and sites that block server-side scraping. If you can read it, the bookmarklet can extract it.
